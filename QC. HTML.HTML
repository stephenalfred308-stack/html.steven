What is Quality Control?

Quality Control (QC) is the process of ensuring that products or services meet defined standards.

Key Goals

  • Consistency
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Defect reduction

Quality Control Principles

1. Standardization

Maintaining consistent processes and procedures.

2. Continuous Improvement

Always looking for ways to improve quality.

3. Data-Driven Decisions

Using measurements and data to guide improvements.

Why QC Matters

It reduces costs, improves reliability, and builds trust with customers.
